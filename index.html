About ROTECT

The problem that we want to solve

  • The risk of a road accident by unknown cracks.
  • The high cost of road crack surveying.

The advantages of using our app

  • Increasing the efficiency of detecting the road crack.
  • Decreasing the cost of identifying the road crack.
  • Decreasing the emission emitted as a result of transportation for surveying the road.
  • Indirectly reducing the accident caused by the road crack.
